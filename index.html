Assignment 1: Animation

This showcases the sprite that I created for my group project and it's full range of animations. The Plague Doctor has animations to walk, shoot and idle in four directions. Shooting creates a new entity with its own animations. If aggroed on a player, the Plague Doctor's eyes will flash while walking. Dying while facing left,right or down triggers one death animation and dying while facing up plays another.

Controls:

  • W,S,A,D - walk directionally
  • SPACE - fire spell (no movements are allowed until the spell finishes firing)
  • I - Stop current animation and stands idle
  • O: turn on/off the aggro animations while walking (the eye flashing is very faint)
  • K: Play death animation and remove entity from world (Doing this will require resetting the page to bring the Plague Doctor back)
